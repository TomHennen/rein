      agent/<n>/<nonce>
  where <n> is the declared issue number and <nonce> is a short name you choose
  (letters/digits, then letters/digits/./_/-). Any other ref is rejected.
- One issue per push.

NETWORK

- Every other host is unreachable. A failed fetch is the sandbox, not a flaky network.
